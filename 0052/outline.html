Each week we will cover 2 broad topics in the lectures and tutorial based on the topics you have covered in the lectures.

Week 1

  1. Economic System
    ESPP, Ch1: Capitalism and Democracy

Students learn about about how a capitalistic economy works i.e., how firms and households buy and sell in the market.

  1. Social interaction
    ESPP, Ch2: Social Interactions and Economic Outcomes

Students learn about how individual agency, i.e., choice people make in a social interaction, can lead to different kinds of social problem.

Week 2

  1. Criterion for Fairness and Efficiency | ESPP, Ch3: Public Policy for Fairness and Efficiency

The lecture introduces the students to the criteria economist use to evaluate fairness and efficiency.

  1. Introduction to Labour Economics | ESPP, Ch4: Work, Wellbeing and, Scarcity

The students learn how individual make choices about work and leisure.

Week 3

  1. Institutional Economics | ESPP, Ch 5: Institutions, Power and, Inequality

The student are introduced to institutional economics. This topic builds on the topic on social interaction.

  1. What is a firm? An Economic Perspective | ESPP, Ch6: The firm: Employees, managers, and owners

Students learn what constitutes a firm in the modern economy.

Week 4

  1. Market Structure | ESPP, Ch7: Firms and markets for goods and services

The students learn the how a market with product differentiation works.

  1. Banks: Borrowers and Lenders | ESPP, Ch9: The credit market: Borrowers, lenders, and the rate of interest

The students learn how banks are designed and the role bank borrowers and lenders play in the banking system.

Week 5

  1. Financial System | ESPP, Ch10: Banks, money, housing, and financial assets

Students learn how the financial system works and role it played in the Great Depression and the Financial Crisis.

  1. Market Successes and Failures | ESPP, Ch11: Market successes and failures

The students learn about the conditions under which market fails and the role policy can play in ensuring that the market works.
