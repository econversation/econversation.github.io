Markdown footnotes become side notes.[^long] After the note. This paragraph is not short, but the margin note next to us is much longer, which pushes the subsequent notes further away from their anchor. But also, footnotes.[^foot]

[^foot]:
  {.} Use a prefix of `{.}` to leave it as a footnote. Unfortunately, side notes and margin notes don't share the same namespace. If you want to mix side notes and footnotes in the same document, you might want to use margin notes, so that there is only one kind of numbered note.
