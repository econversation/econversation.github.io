Economic Growth

A course on modern economic growth.

 

 

An gentle introduction to economic growth for the second year undergraduates.

Each lecture is accompanied by a worksheet. The worksheets contain incomplete diagrams for students to complete during the lecture and serve as a tool for active learning. The tutorials and the math exercises are designed to familiarise the students with the technical aspects of the course.
